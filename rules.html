Space Invaders Game Rules

Game Controls & Rules:

  • Move Your Ship: Use Left/Right Arrow keys or swipe to navigate your ship horizontally across the screen.
  • Shoot to Destroy: Press Spacebar (or tap) to shoot and destroy enemy alien ships.
  • Target Red Alien Ships: Focus on destroying red alien ships to score points and keep the battlefield clear.
  • Prevent Alien Invasion: Avoid letting alien ships reach the bottom of the screen, or it's game over!
  • Endless Survival Challenge: Survive for as long as possible by outmaneuvering enemies and staying alive.

Power-Up Benefits:

  • Health Boost: Collect Health Boost power-ups to increase lives and last longer in the game.
  • Speed Boost: Grab the Speed Boost to enhance your movement speed, making it easier to dodge enemies.
  • Shield Power-Up: Activate the Shield to become invincible for a short period and attack without fear.

Score Table

Earn points by defeating different types of alien ships. Each type has its unique score value:

Cute Alien 15 Points
