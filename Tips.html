Space Shooter Tips & Strategies

Basic Gameplay Tips

  • Always keep moving to avoid enemy fire.
  • Focus on clearing one side of the screen before engaging the other side.
  • Watch the enemies’ movement patterns to predict their next position.

Enemy Guide

Green Alien

Quickly dodges side-to-side and is hard to hit. Try to hit them while they're switching directions.

Pink Bug

Moves slowly but has higher health. Use rapid shots to take them down faster.

Ghost Enemy

Floats towards you at a constant speed. Position yourself to shoot it straight on.

Dodging Tips

  • Use quick taps to move your ship incrementally and dodge bullets.
  • Stay away from the screen edges to avoid getting trapped by enemy fire.
  • If you see multiple enemies moving towards you, weave through them to avoid collision.

Shooting Strategies

  • Aim for clusters of enemies to maximize your shot's effectiveness.
  • Take out enemies with predictable patterns first to clear space.
  • Use your ship’s rapid-fire ability when enemies are grouped close together.
